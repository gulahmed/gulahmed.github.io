Object Oriented Programming - Namal College

The course is spread over the duration of 14 to 15 weeks. We will start by exploring the basic constructs provided in the C++ programming language for procedural and object-oriented programming. Our main foucus will be developing a general understanding of object oriented way of constructing programs but we will try to incorporate modern C++ ecosystem and best practises. By the end of the semester, you will be equipped to use this powerful language and its surrounding community as a tool to solve any meaningful problems you decide to tackle in the future!
